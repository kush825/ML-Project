Dataset Architecture

Clinical Input Schema

The underlying data framework used to train our cardiovascular forecasting ensemble.

Chronological Age

Captured in days and normalized for model ingestion.

Stature

Patient height measured in centimeters (cm).

Body Mass

Patient weight measured in kilograms (kg).

Gender Identity

Categorical encoding of biological sex.

Systolic Pressure

Arterial pressure during heart contraction phase (ap_hi).

Diastolic Pressure

Arterial pressure between heart beats (ap_lo).

Cholesterol Profile

Categorical Level: 1 (Normal), 2 (Elevated), 3 (High).

Glucose Concentration

Categorical Level: 1 (Normal), 2 (Elevated), 3 (High).

Tobacco Usage

Binary indicator for active smoking habits.

Alcohol Intake

Binary indicator for regular alcohol consumption.

Physical Activity

Binary indicator for regular physical exercise.
